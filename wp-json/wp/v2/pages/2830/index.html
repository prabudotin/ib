The COVID-19 pandemic has severely affected the TVET system. Institutions can’t open and there’s no training happening at all.<\/p>

Our initiative helps TVET institutions in the developing world enhance their capabilities to fight this difficult situation. iBee provides many learning models to suit the needs of the organization. iBee also provides value added services for content creation including gamified animated content with AR\/VR capabilities.\u00a0<\/p><\/div><\/div><\/div><\/div>\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
