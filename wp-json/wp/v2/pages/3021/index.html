Evidence suggests that the likelihood of pandemics has increased over the past century owing to increased global travel and integration, urbanization, changes in land use, and greater exploitation of the natural environment. We believe we could use technology to alleviate the ramifications of pandemics. Some of the areas we’re working on are:<\/p>

  • Pandemic Portal and Mobile platform for governments<\/li>
  • Integrated Visitor management with Facial recognition based track and trace capabilities for enterprises and office complexes<\/li>
  • X-ray based COVID tracking for public health authorities<\/li>
  • Inspection solution for public places and places of worship<\/li>
