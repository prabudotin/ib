Welcome to our Privacy Policy<\/h3>

Your privacy is critically important to us.<\/h6>

IBee Tech Labs is located at:<\/p>

Dubai World Central, Business Center,
PO Box 390 667
Dubai \u2013 UAE<\/p>

It is iBee Tech Labs’s policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect while operating our website. This Privacy Policy applies to ibeetechlabs.com\/<\/a> (hereinafter, “us”, “we”, or “ibeetechlabs.com\/”). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting personally identifiable information you may provide us through the Website. We have adopted this privacy policy (“Privacy Policy”) to explain what information may be collected on our Website, how we use this information, and under what circumstances we may disclose the information to third parties. This Privacy Policy applies only to information we collect through the Website and does not apply to our collection of information from other sources.<\/p>

This Privacy Policy, together with the Terms and conditions posted on our Website, set forth the general rules and policies governing your use of our Website. Depending on your activities when visiting our Website, you may be required to agree to additional terms and conditions.<\/p>

Website Visitors<\/h6>

Like most website operators, IBee Tech Labs collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. IBee Tech Labs’s purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how IBee Tech Labs’s visitors use its website. From time to time, IBee Tech Labs may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.<\/p>

IBee Tech Labs also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses for logged in users and for users leaving comments on http:\/\/ibeetechlabs.com\/ blog posts. IBee Tech Labs only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below.<\/p>

Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information<\/h6>

Certain visitors to IBee Tech Labs’s websites choose to interact with IBee Tech Labs in ways that require IBee Tech Labs to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that IBee Tech Labs gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who sign up for a blog at http:\/\/ibeetechlabs.com\/ to provide a username and email address.<\/p>

Security<\/h6>

The security of your Personal Information is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.<\/p>

Advertisements<\/h6>

Ads appearing on our website may be delivered to users by advertising partners, who may set cookies. These cookies allow the ad server to recognize your computer each time they send you an online advertisement to compile information about you or others who use your computer. This information allows ad networks to, among other things, deliver targeted advertisements that they believe will be of most interest to you. This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by IBee Tech Labs and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.<\/p>

Links To External Sites<\/h6>

Our Service may contain links to external sites that are not operated by us. If you click on a third party link, you will be directed to that third party’s site. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy and terms and conditions of every site you visit.<\/p>

We have no control over, and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies or practices of any third party sites, products or services.<\/p>

Ibeetechlabs.com\/ uses Google AdWords for remarketing<\/h6>

Ibeetechlabs.com\/ uses the remarketing services to advertise on third party websites (including Google) to previous visitors to our site. It could mean that we advertise to previous visitors who haven’t completed a task on our site, for example using the contact form to make an enquiry. This could be in the form of an advertisement on the Google search results page, or a site in the Google Display Network. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on someone’s past visits. Of course, any data collected will be used in accordance with our own privacy policy and Google’s privacy policy.<\/p>

You can set preferences for how Google advertises to you using the Google Ad Preferences page, and if you want to you can opt out of interest-based advertising entirely by cookie settings or permanently using a browser plugin.<\/p>

Aggregated Statistics<\/h6>

IBee Tech Labs may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its website. IBee Tech Labs may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, IBee Tech Labs does not disclose your personally-identifying information.<\/p>

Cookies<\/h6>

To enrich and perfect your online experience, IBee Tech Labs uses “Cookies”, similar technologies and services provided by others to display personalized content, appropriate advertising and store your preferences on your computer.<\/p>

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. IBee Tech Labs uses cookies to help IBee Tech Labs identify and track visitors, their usage of http:\/\/ibeetechlabs.com\/, and their website access preferences. IBee Tech Labs visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using IBee Tech Labs’s websites, with the drawback that certain features of IBee Tech Labs’s websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.<\/p>

By continuing to navigate our website without changing your cookie settings, you hereby acknowledge and agree to IBee Tech Labs’s use of cookies.<\/p>

Privacy Policy Changes<\/h6>

Although most changes are likely to be minor, IBee Tech Labs may change its Privacy Policy from time to time, and in IBee Tech Labs’s sole discretion. IBee Tech Labs encourages visitors to frequently check this page for any changes to its Privacy Policy. Your continued use of this site after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.<\/p>
